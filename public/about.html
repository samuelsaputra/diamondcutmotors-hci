About Us

DiamondCut Motors is a prestigious car reseller that provides luxurious vehicles throughout Indonesia. It was founded in 1998 and has been growing exponentially ever since. DiamondCut Motors offers various renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others. A number of business owners and successful individuals have been customers of DiamondCut Motors, drawn by its cutting-edge service, timely delivery, and vehicle safety.


At DiamondCut Motors, our vision is simple: to redefine automotive excellence and deliver unparalleled luxury experiences to our customers. We strive to be the premier destination for those seeking not just a car, but an embodiment of sophistication and prestige on wheels.


Our mission is to curate a selection of the world's most prestigious automotive brands, including Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and more, ensuring that every vehicle that leaves our showroom represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering and craftsmanship. We're committed to exceeding the expectations of our clientele by providing exceptional service, timely delivery, and uncompromising attention to detail.


Throughout our journey, we've had the privilege of serving a diverse clientele, including business owners and successful individuals who demand nothing but the best. They've chosen DiamondCut Motors for our commitment to excellence, our dedication to safety, and our unwavering passion for the automotive industry.
